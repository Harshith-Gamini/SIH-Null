About

Welcome to the 24-hour cyber security hackathon, an event designed to bring together the brightest minds in the field of cybersecurity to collaborate, innovate, and create cutting-edge solutions to keep our nation safe from cyber threats.
Over the next 24 hours, participants will work in teams to develop innovative cybersecurity solutions, using the latest technologies and techniques to address a range of cyber threats. These could include network security, data protection, encryption, threat detection and response, and much more.
The goal of this hackathon is to create a platform for young talent to showcase their skills and come up with innovative solutions that can help keep our nation safe from cyber threats. The top teams from this hackathon will be nominated from our university to participate in the national-level hackathon Kavach where they will compete against other top teams from across the country.
So, get ready to push your limits, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and create solutions that can make a real difference in keeping our nation's cyber infrastructure secure. Good luck to all the participants!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Rakshak?

Rakshak is a 24-hour hackathon with 20 different Problem Statements. The Top performers will be representing VIT-AP in the national Level hackathon Kavach.

What is Kavach?

Kavach is a national level hackathon for the bright minds brought by MoE's Innovation Cell, AICTE along with Bureau of Police Research Development (BPR&D)(MHA) and Indian Cybercrime Coordination Centre (I4C)(MHA). For knowing more about Kavach you can visit: Kavach

What will be the schedule for Rakshak?

Rakshak is a 24-hour hackathon starting from 27th April, 2023, 9:00 AM till 28th April, 2023, 11:30 AM. For checking the complete schedule, please visit schedule

Where will be the Problem Statements for the hackathon?

As Rakshak is a qualifying internal hackathon for Kavach, we are allowing students to select any one Kavach's Problem Statement. You can see the Problem Statement here: Statements

What if I can't miss the class due to low attendance?

For the hackathon period, each participant will be getting On duty, provided, he/she is present full time in the hackathon.

How can I register for the Hackathon?

You can register yourself here on our website.

What is the deadline for the registration?

For accomodation and food service, we advise you to register before 26th April, 2023 - 10:00 AM. Participants registering after that won't be eligible for accomodation and food service.

Do you have a code of conduct?

Yes! We will be following the VIT-AP University's code of conduct.
